🧘 Tai Chi Steps

Draft outline — Paul will review and update with the exact sequence taught in class.

Warm-Up

  1. Shoulder and arm circles
  2. Gentle neck stretches
  3. Weight shifting side to side
  4. Knee circles and ankle rotations

Basic Movements

  1. Commencement — arms rise and fall with breath
  2. Open Arms / Hold the Ball
  3. Wave Hands Like Clouds
  4. Brush Knee and Push
  5. Grasp the Bird's Tail

Closing

  1. Gather Qi to center
  2. Slow, deep breaths
  3. Closing posture
