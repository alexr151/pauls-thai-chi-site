About Me

Diagnosed with heart failure in 2002, I needed an exercise that was gentle yet challenging. After trying to learn tai chi with five different instructors at four different schools, I finally found Tai Chi for Arthritis at the Woodbury YMCA in about 2011.

In 2012, I took the training and became a certified Tai Chi for Arthritis instructor, and a certified Group Fitness Instructor. I’ve been teaching Tai Chi for Arthritis ever since. Later, I learned Tai Ji Quan/Movement for Better Balance (TJQ/MMB), and teach that twice a week at the Lake Elmo and Stillwater Libraries. I have a regular class at the Hudson YMCA, and I’ve taught at several YMCAs around the Twin Cities. I’ve taught over a thousand classes.
